WHAT IS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Information Technology, often abbreviated as IT, refers to the utilization of computers, software, networks, and digital technology to store, transmit, retrieve, and process data and information.
It encompasses a wide range of activities related to managing and using technology to meet the needs of individuals, businesses, and organizations. IT plays a crucial role in various aspects of modern life,
from communication and data storage to decision-making, automation, and problem-solving.

What is "Artificial Intelligence"


⚝ Artificial intelligence

Artificial Intelligence, refers to the development of computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding natural language, and perceiving their environment. AI is a multidisciplinary field that combines computer science, mathematics, psychology, linguistics, and other disciplines to create intelligent machines.

There are various subfields within AI, including:


• Machine Learning:

This involves training computer algorithms to learn from and make predictions or decisions based on data. Machine learning is widely used in applications like recommendation systems, image and speech recognition, and predictive analytics

• Deep learning

A subset of machine learning, deep learning uses artificial neural networks with multiple layers (deep neural networks) to analyze and interpret data. It's especially powerful in tasks like image and speech recognition.

Robotics

AI plays a crucial role in the development of robots and autonomous systems that can perform tasks in various environments, such as manufacturing, healthcare, and exploration.
